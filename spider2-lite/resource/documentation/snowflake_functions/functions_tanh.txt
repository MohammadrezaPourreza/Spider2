  TANH  ¶

Computes the hyperbolic tangent of its argument.

##  Syntax  ¶

    
    
    TANH( <real_expr> )
    

Copy

##  Arguments  ¶

` _ real_expr  _ `

    

This expression should evaluate to a real number.

##  Examples  ¶

>
>     SELECT TANH(1.5);
>  
>     --------------+
>       TANH(1.5)   |
>     --------------+
>      0.9051482536 |
>     --------------+
>  
>
> Copy
