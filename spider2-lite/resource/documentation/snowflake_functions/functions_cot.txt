  COT  ¶

Computes the cotangent of its argument; the argument should be expressed in
radians.

##  Syntax  ¶

    
    
    COT( <real_expr> )
    

Copy

##  Examples  ¶

>
>     SELECT COT(0), COT(PI()/3), COT(RADIANS(90));
>  
>     --------+--------------+------------------+
>      COT(0) | COT(PI()/3)  | COT(RADIANS(90)) |
>     --------+--------------+------------------+
>      inf    | 0.5773502692 | 6.123233996e-17  |
>     --------+--------------+------------------+
>  
>
> Copy
