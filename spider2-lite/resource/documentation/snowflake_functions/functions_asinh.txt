  ASINH  ¶

Computes the inverse (arc) hyperbolic sine of its argument.

##  Syntax  ¶

    
    
    ASINH( <real_expr> )
    

Copy

##  Arguments  ¶

` _ real_expr  _ `

    

This expression should evaluate to a real number.

##  Examples  ¶

>
>     SELECT ASINH(2.129279455);
>  
>     --------------------+
>      ASINH(2.129279455) |
>     --------------------+
>      1.5                |
>     --------------------+
>  
>
> Copy
