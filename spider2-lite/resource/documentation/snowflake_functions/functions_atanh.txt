  ATANH  ¶

Computes the inverse (arc) hyperbolic tangent of its argument.

##  Syntax  ¶

    
    
    ATANH( <real_expr> )
    

Copy

##  Arguments  ¶

` _ real_expr  _ `

    

This expression should evaluate to a real number between -1.0 and +1.0
(inclusive).

##  Examples  ¶

>
>     SELECT ATANH(0.9051482536);
>  
>     ---------------------+
>      ATANH(0.9051482536) |
>     ---------------------+
>      1.5                 |
>     ---------------------+
>  
>
> Copy
