  COSH  ¶

Computes the hyperbolic cosine of its argument.

##  Syntax  ¶

    
    
    COSH( <real_expr> )
    

Copy

##  Arguments  ¶

` _ real_expr  _ `

    

This expression should evaluate to a real number.

##  Examples  ¶

>
>     SELECT COSH(1.5);
>  
>     -------------+
>       COSH(1.5)  |
>     -------------+
>      2.352409615 |
>     -------------+
>  
>
> Copy
